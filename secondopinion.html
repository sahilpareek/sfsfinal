How to Join SFS and Submit Your Case

1

Download the SFS App

Get the official Surgeons for Surgeons app from Google Play or Apple App Store

2

Register Your Account

Sign up, verify your identity and become a confirmed SFS member

3

Complete Your Profile

Add speciality, qualifications and alma mater to connect with relevant groups

4

Join Relevant Groups

Join relevant specialty or interest groups for focused discussions

5

Post Your Case for Review

Share cases securely for peer feedback or a second opinion

6

Interact & Collaborate

Comment on cases, share insights and network with surgeons worldwide

7

Stay Updated

Enable alerts for cases, discussions and resources
